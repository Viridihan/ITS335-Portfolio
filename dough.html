Dough Recipe

The first step in creating this pie is creating the dough, as it will need time to rest before moving on to the next step.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ½ vanilla bean
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup salted butter, ice cold
  • ½ cup butter flavor shortening, cubed & ice cold
  • 6 tablespoons buttermilk, chilled

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl combine flour, sugar, vanilla bean, and salt.
  2. Add butter and shortening and then work between fingers until the mixture resembles coarse meal.
  3. Drizzle in ice-cold buttermilk and continue working to form dough.
  4. Shape the dough into a disc and wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 2 to 24 hours.
