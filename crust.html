Crust Recipe

Once the dough has chilled for enough time, the next step would be to create the crust and bake it.

Ingredients

  • Dough Disc
  • Egg wash

Instructions

  1. Roll it out to 1/4 inch thick and place into pie pan. Remove the excess.
  2. Roll out dough excess and cut out small squares.
  3. Brush egg wash along the edges and then arrange the dough squares in a pixelated design.
  4. Prick the bottom and inside edges of the pie crust with a fork.
  5. Cover the pie with plastic wrap and then fill it with uncooked beans. Wrap the beans with the excess plastic. Egg wash edges of the crust.
  6. Bake for 35 minutes until golden brown around the edges.
  7. Remove beans and bake another 10 to 15 minutes.
